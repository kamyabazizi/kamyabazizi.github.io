About me

Kamyab Azizi

Electrical and Electronics Engineer / AI Researcher

I was born on 19 November 1996 in Mahabad, Iran. I have studied Electrical and Electronics Engineering at Amirkabir University-Tehran Polytechnic and I've received my B.Sc degrees from Amirkabir University. Currently, I'm working as a research assistant under the supervision of Dr.Hassan Taheri. My dissertation is based on a project for Model Compression and Acceleration for Deep Neural Networks to deploy them on devices with limited computational and memory resources.
